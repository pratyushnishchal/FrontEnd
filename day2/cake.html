Simple Sponge Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g castor sugar
  • 85g self-rasing flour

Notes on Ingredients

Castor Sugar
Finely granuated white sugar
self-rasing flour
A pre- mixed combination of flour and leavening agent

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 190oC
  2. Grease a 20 cm round cake pan
  3. In a medium bowl, whip together the eggs and castor sugar until fluffy
  4. Fold in flour
  5. Pour mixture into the prepared pan
  6. Bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven or until the top of the cake springs back when lightly pressed
  7. Cool in the pan oven a wire rack
